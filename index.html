Questions? We've got answers.

Frequently Asked by Furkid Parents

How often can I bathe my furkid?
It's gentle enough for regular baths! Once every 1–2 weeks works great, or whenever your furkid needs a refresh.
Is it okay for dry or sensitive skin?
No Ruff Days is made with thoughtful, plant-based ingredients like aloe, oat, and hyaluronic acid, chosen with skin comfort in mind.
Does it lather well without sulphates?
Yes! You'll still get a soft, creamy lather. Just add a splash of water and massage it in gently.
Why does my furkid's coat feel like conditioner was used?

That's the magic of our nourishing formula! With hydrating hyaluronic acid and plant-based oils, the coat may feel extra silky after rinsing. Don't worry — it's not residue, just healthy hydration.

As long as you rinse thoroughly, that soft finish means your furkid's coat is moisturised and cuddle-ready.

One shampoo for both cats and dogs?
Yes! No Ruff Days is pH-balanced at 6.6 and made without essential oils. Suitable for both cats and dogs, even those with more sensitive skin.
What about puppies and kittens?
We recommend using it once your furkid is over 12 weeks old. For younger ones, always check with your vet before trying anything new.
Does it have any added fragrance?
Nope! Our formula is 100% no added fragrance and essential oil-free. We don't mask odour, we gently remove them.
Is it tear-free?
It's made to be mild, but like all shampoos, it's best to avoid the eye area.
